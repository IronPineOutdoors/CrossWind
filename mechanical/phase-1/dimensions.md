# Phase 1 Dimensions

- Base: 28" x 28" x 3/4" plywood.
- Rotating plate: 20" x 20" plywood.
- Bearing: 12" lazy susan bearing centered between base and plate.

## Suggested Layout

- Place the battery near the rear or side to help keep the base planted.
- Place the electronics box where cables can reach motor, switches, and controls without crossing the linkage.
- Mount the wiper motor to the fixed base.
- Keep the linkage below or outside the thrower footprint.
- Leave room for adjustable thrower rails on the top plate.
