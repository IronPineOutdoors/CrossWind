# Phase 1 Cut List

## Plywood Cuts

- One 28" x 28" base from 3/4" plywood.
- One 20" x 20" rotating top plate.

## Motor Block

- One temporary motor mounting block sized to the Mitsubishi Outlander rear wiper motor.
- Shim stock or spacer blocks for alignment during linkage tests.

## Temporary Switch Brackets

- Two adjustable brackets for roller limit switches.
- Slots are preferred over fixed holes until sweep angle is proven.

## Optional Stake Tabs

- Four small tabs or corner blocks can be added for ground stakes or tie-downs.
